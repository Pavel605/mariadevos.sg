I'm a serial entrepreneur, builder and mother of three based in Asia, after living and working across three continents.

As CEO and co-founder of Nino Nana, I'm building Singapore's #1 independent baby diaper brand on a simple conviction: performance, non-toxic materials and environmental responsibility don't have to be a trade-off.

We're growing across markets and actively looking to partner with aligned distributors — anywhere in the world. If you're in FMCG and believe in better-for-you products, I'd love to talk.

I'm building alongside three kids — and trying to make my network a little less male-heavy along the way.

The journey

A track record of building.

From the world's largest youth-run organisation to founding consumer brands across Asia and Europe.

2021 — Present · Singapore
CEO & Co-founder · Nino Nana Now

Eco-conscious baby diapers designed in Singapore — premium comfort with respect for the planet, built from the safest materials handpicked worldwide. Totally Chlorine Free certified, dermatologically tested, and called "the Ferrari of diapers" across thousands of reviews. Now growing across Asia.

2005 — 2021 · Singapore, Europe & Central Asia
Earlier · Founder, operator & leader

Before Nino Nana: Managing Partner at Eco Trade International, bringing premium mother & baby FMCG brands to Eastern Europe & Central Asia; CMO & Co-founder of Clubvivre, Singapore's #1 on-demand chef service featured on TV, radio & print; Founder of EvenPanda; Business Development at ACG Global; and President, then Regional Director at AIESEC — leading operations across 26 countries and 13,000 people.

Education — MSc International Business & BA International Economics, St. Petersburg State University of Economics & Finance · The Founder Institute.
